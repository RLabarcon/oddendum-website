In a Nutshell

Nov 4, 2015


Summary:

A series of well-researched, short, educational videos produced by the folks at Munich design studio Kurzgesagt, which itself means “In a Nutshell” in Deutsch.

The studio publishes around one video per month, taking the time to condense the forefront of human knowledge into concise, informative, videos skinned with slick animation. The studio's bar for quality is increasingly high, and has, on occasion, prompted the team to scrap entire projects because they just weren't "good enough."

The channel had been called by its German moniker until recently. It's since been replaced with a more English version of the idiom.

According to this video, it's because "Kurzgesagt" is very difficult to pronounce.


Here are a few of their videos that I personally recommend, covering a variety of topics.


The value in Kurzgesagt (and similar projects), I think, is that they make knowing about the state of things a little bit easier. They pack a lot of downright incredible information that everyone should know about into short videos in terms that everyone can understand.

Also, the colorful animation and conversational tone lend a sheen of watchability to lectures on otherwise-unwieldy concepts in geopolitics and astrophysics, which is nice.

ENJOY.

(If you did, be sure to subscribe to their channel for more.)
